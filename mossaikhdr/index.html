Spectrum HDR is an image editor designed to make the most powerful professional image editing tools accessible to everyone. And with an extensive collection of tools for editing and retouching photos, and adding stunning effects, it’s the only HDR image editor you’ll need.

Experience

Totally and completely Mac.

Spectrum HDR is a powerful, beautiful, and easy to use image editor. Featuring an elegant and streamlined single-window interface, it puts all the focus on the images you edit, rather than what's around them. And with a design that is totally and completely Mac, it feels instantly familiar from the moment you first open the app. So image editing becomes simpler and more enjoyable than ever.

Layout

Everything you need to create great experiences.

Designed to give the best experience from the moment you open, Spectrum HDR considers the importance of space, and provides a single-window interface for all the tools. The editor also uses handy keyboard shortcuts to ease the out the process of editing your favourite HDR moments.

Color Adjustments

Every color adjustment for picture-perfect photos.

Featuring a collection of powerful, nondestructive color adjustments, Spectrum HDR lets you edit the colors in your photos in any way you want. Each adjustment has breathtaking color quality thanks to advanced image processing techniques that eliminate posterization artifacts and improve clarity. The Batch Processing feature lets you edit multiple RAW images as single documents using all the additional image detail saved in your RAW files. And Presets give you a way to easily save, reuse, and share your favorite adjustment combinations.

Effects

Real-time, nondestructive, blockbuster effects.

Spectrum HDR comes with a collection of versatile effects that you can mix and combine to develop any artistic or special effects you can imagine. They’re nondestructive, so you can always edit, rearrange, and remove the effects you’ve applied, and even apply multiple effects to a single layer — seeing all your changes in real time.

Technologies

Machine learning-enhanced, GPU-powered image editing.

The incredibly modern image editing engine in Spectrum HDR is seriously sophisticated and seriously powerful. It’s powered by the graphics processor in your Mac, in tandem with advanced Mac graphics technologies such as Metal 2 and Core Image. Pushing the boundaries of image editing, Spectrum HDR also includes machine learning-enhanced editing features. So tools are not just blistering fast — they’re whip smart too.
